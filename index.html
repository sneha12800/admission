Eligibility Criteria

Find the specific requirements for your chosen program before you apply.

B.Tech & Integrated M.Tech (1st Year)

  • Passed 10+2 Science examination with Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects along with one of Chemistry/Biotechnology/Biology/Technical Vocational subject.
  • *For Biotechnology Engineering, Mathematics is not compulsory; the student should have Biology/Biotechnology as one of the subjects in 10+2 Science.
  • Minimum 45% marks (40% for reserved category).
  • Must appear for JEE-Main conducted by CBSE.

Lateral Entry (2nd Year)

  • Diploma Holders: Passed 3-year diploma in Engineering/Technology with a minimum of 45% (40% for SC/ST).
  • B.Sc / +3 Sc Students: Passed 3-year Bachelor’s Degree in Science with a minimum of 45% and passed XII with Mathematics.
  • Must appear for O-JEE.

PG Programs (M.Tech, MCA, MBA)

  • M.Tech: B.Tech/M.Sc in relevant field with minimum 50% (45% for SC/ST).
  • MCA/MBA: Bachelor’s Degree (min 3 years) with minimum 50% (45% for SC/ST). MCA requires Mathematics at 10+2 or Graduate level.
  • *There is no age limit for admission to MCA or MBA courses.
  • Must appear for O-JEE.
